AKAN NAMES

Akan names are derived from ghanaian culture

Children,both male and female,are given Akan names based on the day of the week they were born:

    names given to the male species include:
    1. Sunday: Kwasi
    2. Monday: Kwadwo
    3. Tuesday: Kwabena
    4. Wednesday: Kwaku
    5. Thursday: Yaw
    6. Friday: Kofi
    7. Saturday: Kwame
    Names given to female species include:
  1. Sunday: Akosua
  2. Monday: Adwoa
  3. Tuesday: Abenaa
  4. Wednesday: Akua
  5. Thursday: Yaa
  6. Friday: Afua
  7. Saturday: Ama
