AKAN NAMES

Akan names are derived from ghanaian culture

Children,both male and female,are given Akan names based on the day of the week they were born:

    Some names given to the male species include:
    1. Sunday: Kwasi
    2. Monday: Kwadwo
    3. Tuesday: Kwabena
    4. Wednesday: Kwaku
    5. Thursday: Yaw
    6. Friday: Kofi
    7. Saturday: Kwame
        8. Names given to female species include:
    8. Sunday: Akosua
    9. Monday: Adwoa
    10. Tuesday: Abenaa
    11. Wednesday: Akua
    12. Thursday: Yaa
    13. Friday: Afua
    14. Saturday: Ama
